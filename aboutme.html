Vishwas Aravind

Photographer & Visual Storyteller

I’m a photographer drawn to the stark interplay of light and shadow, working primarily in black and white to uncover the silent tension in ordinary scenes. My style is minimalist, often inspired by geometry, contrast, and solitude — echoing the visual storytelling of artists like Ralph Gibson.

I shoot both digital and film (with a Nikon FA), and my current focus is on urban environments and transitional spaces — where chaos, symmetry, and mood collide. I’m also an engineer, which explains the obsession with structure and visual balance. When I’m not behind the lens, I’m building production systems or animating motorsport machines in Blender.
